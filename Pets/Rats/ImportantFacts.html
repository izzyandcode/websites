• Rats are social creatures and I advice you to get a pair or group of the same sex, so that one won't get lonely. Additionally, rats mate very quickly with 2 or more of different sex. A female can be pregnant only 24 hours after giving birth.
  • They need cleaning out everyday
  • You need to change out the bedding and supplies in their cage weekly
  • Don't start holding them for the first one - two days when you have got them
  • Approach them kindly and make sure them aren't too upset
  • Never hold them too tightly
  • Register them for a vet as soon as possible
